Tourists Places

Muhammad Cheng Hoo Mosque

Cheng Ho Mosque in Surabaya was built as a symbol of the acculturation of Chinese and Islamic culture to commemorate Admiral Cheng Ho's expedition and spread of Islam in the archipelago in the 15th century.

House of Sampoerna

The House of Sampoerna is a tobacco museum and headquarters of Sampoerna located in Surabaya. The main building's architectural style was influenced by a Dutch colonial-style compound that was built in 1862 and is now a preserved historical site.

10th Nopember Museum

A well-maintained historical monument and with a museum about the bravery of the people of Surabaya to fight for Indonesia's independence.

Sacred Heart of Jesus Cathedral Church Surabaya

The Church of the Sacred Heart of Jesus was built in 1921 as a place of worship for Catholics during the Dutch colonial era, making it one of the oldest church in Surabaya
